ABOUT THE PROJECT

Key features of the Trading-Simulator

Free

Buying crypto on Trading-Simulator is completely free! That is what makes it risk free and enjoyable for beginners. You are trading with tokens instead of your financial stability.

Learn

With Trading-Simulator you can learn the basics of trading crypto. You can find tutorials, articles and descriptions about everything!

Design

This page was designed to be easy for beginners to adjust to. Everyone can find what they are looking for, except if it is losing money as a beginner.

Responsive

Our site is designed to fit every screen perfetly. This way you can trade anywhere, anytime and on any of your smart devices.
